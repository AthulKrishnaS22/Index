FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What type of projects do you take on? +
I primarily work on UI/UX design for web and mobile apps, including design strategy, wireframing, prototyping, and front-end development. I love early-stage products and redesigns.
Do you design both mobile apps and websites? +
Yes! I design for both platforms. I ensure designs are responsive and optimised for each screen size, providing a consistent and enjoyable experience.
What design tools do you use? +
Figma is my primary tool. I also use Adobe XD, Canva, and Photoshop. For prototyping I prefer Figma's built-in tools for interactive prototypes.
What is the typical timeline for a project? +
Timelines vary based on project scope. A simple landing page may take 1–2 weeks, while a full app design can take 4–8 weeks. I always share a clear timeline upfront.
Can you redesign existing apps? +
Absolutely. Redesigns are something I enjoy — analysing what's not working, understanding user pain points, and crafting a better, more intuitive experience.
